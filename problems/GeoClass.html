Rishikesh is trying to complete a geometry project! He has to draw a right triangle and write an analysis on the same paper. He wants to minimize the size of the analysis, so he decides to make the triangle take up as much space as possible.

Given the lengths of the two legs of the right triangle, find its area.

Input

The first and only line of input contains $l$ and $w$ $(1 \le l, w \le 10^3)$.

Output

Print a single number: the area of the right triangle.
Sample Input
3 3 Sample Output
4.5
Sample Input
3 4 Sample Output
6

Subtask 1: No additional constraints
