Vikram is given an expression on a math test, but he is too lazy to evaluate it.

The expression is of the form $A^B\cdot C+D$. Given the integers $A$, $B$, $C$, and $D$, please determine the result of the expression.

Input

The first and only line of input contains the integers $A$, $B$, $C$, and $D (1 \le A, C, D \le 50, 1 \le B \le 4)$ - the integers.

Output

Print a single integer: the result of the expression.

Sample Input
2 3 4 5 Sample Output
37

Note

$2^3=8$
$8\cdot4=32$
$32+5=37$

Subtask 1: $B=1$
Subtask 2: No additional constraints
